# Coarse Imaging 

The coarse imaging is performed to capture all the (positive) clean components at a low resolution (1 arcsec), which is then zoomed into for the following fine imaging at high resolution (0.2 arcsec). See [Asanok+2023](https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.3847/1538-4357/aca6f1) for more details. The black boxes denote the regions for the fine imaging.
